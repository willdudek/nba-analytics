# NBA Player Analytics

Four seasons of NBA data (2022–23 through 2025–26), explored across scoring trends, shooting efficiency, hustle metrics, and game-level analysis.

Built with Evidence.dev + MotherDuck + dbt.

---

## Season Aggregate Dashboards

- [Scoring Trends](/scoring-trends) — Year-over-year PPG and eFG% with delta indicators
- [3-Point Shooting](/three-point-shooting) — Top shooters by efficiency and volume across seasons
- [Dawg Energy Index](/dawg-energy) — Guard hustle metric tracking ORB, STL, and BLK

## Game-Level Dashboards

- [Player vs Opponent](/player-vs-opponent) — Average stats against specific opponents across all seasons
- [In-Season Trends](/season-trends) — Monthly performance trends averaged across all seasons

---

*Data source: Basketball Reference (season aggregates), NBA Stats API (game logs) | Seasons: 2022-23 through 2025-26*
