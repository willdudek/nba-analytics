# NBA Player Analytics

Three seasons of Basketball Reference data (2022–23 through 2024–25), explored across scoring trends, shooting efficiency, and hustle metrics.

Built with Evidence.dev + MotherDuck.

---

## Dashboards

- [Scoring Trends](/scoring-trends) — Year-over-year PPG and eFG% with delta indicators
- [3-Point Shooting](/three-point-shooting) — Top shooters by efficiency and volume across seasons
- [Dawg Energy Index](/dawg-energy) — Guard hustle metric tracking ORB, STL, and BLK

---

*Data source: Basketball Reference | Seasons: 2022-23, 2023-24, 2024-25*
